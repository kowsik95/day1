HTTP 1.1
1.HTTP1.1 loads resources in a sequential manner (ie) it loads one after the other.
2.Some methods and response codes are used.
3.Keep Alive becomes officially supported.Host header becomes supoorted for virtual domain.

3.Addition of flow control function in units of stream.
